The Safety App

Mobile App to ensure the safety of your beach trip

We know that safety is your top priority both your close ones and yourself. Introducing SANTAI your beach image safety detection. This app brings danger detection into the palm of your hand. Just by aligning your camera to the beach area, this app can detect if that area is a safe place to play and have fun.
